Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

+

VX Software Solutions Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to safeguarding the privacy of users who interact with our V Remind product across all platforms (Web, Android, and iOS). This policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

+

We collect the following categories of data:

Personal Identifiable Information (PII):

  • •Student/Parent/Teacher name, phone number, email ID, profile photo
  • •School name, admin details, class, section, etc.

Usage Data:

  • •Login times, pages viewed, features used

Device Information:

  • •Device model, operating system, IP address, browser type (web), device identifier (mobile)

Location Data (if permitted):

  • •For transport and geo-tracking features

2. How We Use Your Information

+
  • •To create and manage user accounts
  • •To deliver key features such as attendance, timetable, fee details, and academic reporting
  • •To send alerts/notifications via SMS, WhatsApp, email
  • •To generate analytics for academic or administrative improvements
  • •To ensure user authentication and security

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

+

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • •School authorities and staff (internally)
  • •Payment gateways for processing transactions
  • •Third-party services strictly bound by confidentiality, for features like SMS/email

4. Data Retention

+

We retain user data for as long as the school maintains an active subscription. Upon deactivation, data may be archived or deleted as per request.

5. Children's Privacy

+

Our app is meant for educational institutions. Parental consent is considered obtained via school registration. No direct collection is done from children under 13 without institutional consent.

6. Security Measures

+

We implement robust encryption, firewall, and access control measures to protect your data.

7. User Rights

+

Users have the right to:

  • •Access, update, or delete their profile
  • •Withdraw consent
  • •Request data portability or correction via school admin

8. Platform-Specific Permissions

+

Web:

  • •Uses cookies and session-based login

Android/iOS:

  • •May request camera, storage, location, notification permissions for specific features

9. Policy Updates

+

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in-app and on our website. Continued use implies consent.
